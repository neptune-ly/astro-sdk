Credit & Finance ​

Neptune. Astro should implement OpenWave Credit & Finance as a financed-checkout lifecycle over the existing payment, mandate, Open Banking, and webhook surfaces.

Astro must not become a hardcoded credit-score engine. The right architecture is provider-driven:

ComponentResponsibility
CreditAssessmentServiceCreates and refreshes purpose-bound assessments from consented Open Banking data.
FinanceOfferServiceCreates BNPL, revolving-credit, and Murabaha offers.
FinanceContractServiceManages contract activation, cancellation, and status.
RepaymentScheduleServiceBuilds and exposes installment schedules.
CreditDecisionProviderPluggable provider interface for scoring, policy, and lender decisions.

Implementation blueprint ​

flowchart LR
  Merchant["Merchant backend"] --> Astro["Neptune. Astro"]
  Astro --> Consent["Hosted finance consent"]
  Consent --> Bank["Bank Open Banking + SCA"]
  Bank --> Assessment["CreditAssessmentService"]
  Assessment --> Provider["CreditDecisionProvider"]
  Provider --> Offer["FinanceOfferService"]
  Offer --> Acceptance["Hosted / SDK acceptance"]
  Acceptance --> Payment["Payment orchestration"]
  Payment --> Contract["FinanceContractService"]
  Contract --> Schedule["RepaymentScheduleService"]
  Payment --> Webhooks["Signed webhooks"]
LayerAstro responsibilityIntegration responsibility
ConsentHost the customer screen, explain finance scopes, store consent state.Bank exposes OB data and SCA under customer approval.
AssessmentNormalize consented data into an assessment package.Provider returns score, decision, reason codes, and model metadata.
OfferCreate versioned BNPL, revolving, or Murabaha offer records.Provider owns pricing, policy, disclosures, and lender obligations.
AcceptanceKeep acceptance inside hosted or official SDK UI.Merchant must not collect OTP, PIN, or finance credentials.
SettlementReuse normal payment orchestration for financier-to-merchant payment.Bank executes same-bank or interbank route.
ServicingExpose contracts, schedules, webhooks, and audit history.Provider services repayments and customer obligations.

Consent scopes ​

Astro consent screens must show credit-related scopes separately from ordinary account access:

json
[
  "accounts:read",
  "balances:read",
  "transactions:read",
  "credit_assessment:read",
  "income:read",
  "liabilities:read",
  "affordability:read"
]

The visible purpose should say this is for finance eligibility or financed checkout. Do not reuse a general Open Banking consent silently for finance decisions.

Portal surfaces ​

PortalRequired viewPrivacy rule
MerchantFinance option status, final payment status, safe decline reason, webhook history.No raw transaction data.
CustomerConsent, offer, disclosure, accepted contract, repayment schedule, revocation/cancel actions where allowed.Clear finance purpose and repayment obligations.
AdminProvider decision audit, reason codes, correlation IDs, webhook delivery, settlement state.Mask private data unless the admin role explicitly needs it.
BankConsented scopes, selected accounts, SCA state, data-sharing audit.Bank can see only its own customer/account scope.

Hosted acceptance requirements ​

Before acceptance, Astro must show:

  • finance product type
  • merchant and order reference
  • amount, currency, tenor, and cost
  • repayment schedule
  • disclosure or contract URL
  • Murabaha cash price, profit amount, total sale price, and asset description where applicable
  • repayment mandate or scheduled payment method

Acceptance must happen in Astro-hosted or official SDK UI, not in a merchant-owned form.

Build order ​

StepDeliverableTest evidence
1Finance consent UI with credit scopes.Customer rejects consent and no assessment is created.
2CreditDecisionProvider interface and mock provider.Assessment returns model metadata and safe reason codes.
3Finance offer and contract persistence.BNPL and Murabaha offers store disclosure version and schedule.
4Hosted acceptance surface.Acceptance is blocked outside hosted or SDK session.
5Financier-to-merchant payment link.Merchant receives signed final payment webhook only after settlement.
6Customer portal servicing screens.Customer can view contract, schedule, repayments, and cancellation state.

Webhooks ​

Astro SDK receivers should handle:

EventUse
credit_assessment.completedFinance options can be calculated.
finance_offer.createdCustomer can review an offer.
finance_offer.acceptedCustomer accepted terms and schedule.
finance_contract.activeContract is active after merchant payment confirmation.
finance_contract.cancelledContract was cancelled.
repayment.completedInstallment collected.
repayment.failedInstallment failed or was rejected.
