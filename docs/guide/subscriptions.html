Subscriptions ​

Subscriptions in Astro are built on OpenWave recurring mandates. A mandate is permission from the customer to charge a merchant under defined limits.

Mandate lifecycle ​

StateMeaning
PENDING_CONSENTMerchant requested a recurring mandate and the customer must approve it.
ACTIVEMerchant may charge within the approved rules.
SUSPENDEDCharges are temporarily blocked.
CANCELLEDMandate is closed and cannot be charged.
EXPIREDMandate reached its expiry date.

Create a mandate ​

ts
const mandate = await astro.payments.createMandate({
  payer_alias: 'mtellesy@andalus',
  amount_limit: 10000,
  currency: 'LYD',
  frequency: 'MONTHLY',
  description: 'Neptune Store Care+ monthly plan',
  consent_redirect_url: 'https://merchant.example.com/account/subscriptions',
  merchant_reference: 'sub_1001',
}, {
  idempotencyKey: 'sub_1001_create_mandate',
})

The SDK returns consent_url. Send the customer there or open it in the official web/mobile SDK surface.

Customer approval ​

The customer must see a clear mandate summary before approval:

FieldExample
MerchantNeptune Store
Amount10.000 LYD
FrequencyMonthly
First chargeToday or configured start date
Expiry2027-05-08
AccountMasked NPT or IBAN

Approval uses bank SCA when required by policy. The merchant must not approve mandates silently.

Presented subscription approval ​

Subscriptions may start from merchant-presented QR or NFC, but the approval still becomes a normal Astro mandate-consent flow.

mermaid
sequenceDiagram
  autonumber
  participant M as Merchant backend
  participant C as Customer device
  participant A as Neptune. Astro
  participant B as Bank

  M->>A: create mandate presentment
  A-->>M: QR / NFC payload
  C->>A: claim presentment
  A-->>C: show merchant, amount, frequency, expiry
  C->>B: OTP / push / SCA
  B-->>A: approved
  A-->>M: mandate.activated

The important point is that Astro must show the recurring scope clearly before approval. A QR code or tap alone is never enough to activate a subscription.

Charge an active mandate ​

ts
await astro.payments.chargeMandate(mandate.mandate_id, {
  amount: 10000,
  description: 'Care+ monthly plan - May 2026',
  merchant_reference: 'sub-invoice-2026-05',
}, {
  idempotencyKey: 'sub-invoice-2026-05-charge',
})

Astro validates the mandate limits before routing the charge. Failed charge attempts return status = FAILED with error_code and error_message, and emit mandate.charge.failed.

Cancel a mandate ​

Customers should be able to cancel from the Astro customer portal or from the merchant account page. The merchant backend can also cancel when the customer requests cancellation.

ts
await astro.payments.cancelMandate(mandate.mandate_id)

Webhook events ​

EventUse
mandate.activatedActivate subscription in merchant system.
mandate.cancelledStop future service renewal.
mandate.charge.completedMark invoice paid.
mandate.charge.failedNotify customer and retry according to merchant policy.
